Networks and their Components

  • Network is a collection of devices or computers linked together to share information or resources.
  • It can take various form, such as LAN, WAN or the Internet.
  • The purpose of a network is to facilitate the efficient and seamless exchange of data
  • A network can vary in scale from a small home network to large enterprises infrastructures.
